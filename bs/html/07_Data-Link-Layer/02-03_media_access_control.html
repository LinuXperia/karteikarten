Media Access Control
Data Link Layer 2.3
Carrier Sense Multiple Access (CSMA)

detection of other devices trying to send, detection and handling of collistions.

CSMA/Collision Detection (CSMA/CD)

  • devices listen for signals and send if media is free
  • in case of concurrent media access all devices stop to send data and wait a small amount of time
  • used for traditional forms of Ethernet

CSMA/Collision Avoidance (CSMA/CA)

  • devices listen for signals and send a notification of their intent to use the media then they send their data
  • specifies a random backoff procedure for all waiting nodes (the most likely opportunity for medium contention is just after the medium becomes available. making nodes backoff for a random time period reduces the likelihood of collisions.)
  • used by 802.11 wireless networking technologies
